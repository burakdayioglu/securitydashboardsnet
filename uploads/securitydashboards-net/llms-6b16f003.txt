# securitydashboards.net

> A practical, vendor-neutral guide to security dashboards and CISO dashboards: what they are, how the tools compare, and the data layer underneath that decides whether the numbers can be trusted. Written by a security founder with two exits (Innovera, Atar Labs).

The core idea across this site: a dashboard is only a picture of your data. The visualization layer (Grafana, Power BI, ServiceNow, Splunk) is the easy, near-solved part. What decides whether a number is trustworthy and defensible is the data layer beneath it: whether data is collected consistently, normalized, computed deterministically, recorded over time, and traceable to its source. That underlying layer is described as a "security KPI system of record."
